Mobile Prototype • Hiking & Mountains

Plan safer, smarter mountain trips — in one app.

MountGuide is an all-in-one app concept for mountain enthusiasts who want to plan enjoyable hikes with live weather, updated TOPR alerts, interactive maps, an AI assistant for route personalization, and a community to share tips.

The Problem

Hikers often jump between multiple tools: maps, weather apps, alert pages, forums, and messengers. This fragmentation makes planning slower and increases the risk of missing critical updates.

The Solution

MountGuide centralizes planning and safety into one experience — with personalized routes, live data, and a social space for sharing recommendations and trip insights.

Key Features

Short, clear blocks — perfect for an app preview or portfolio landing page.

🗺️ Smart Route Planning

Explore trails, compare difficulty, and plan routes that match your time, fitness level, and goals.

🌦️ Live Weather & TOPR Alerts

Stay up to date with changing conditions. Get real-time weather and updated safety communications.

🤖 AI Hiking Assistant

Personalize your plan: route suggestions, gear tips, safety recommendations, and instant answers.

🏔️ Community & Recommendations

Share trail tips, favorite routes, and trip updates with other mountain enthusiasts.

User Journey

Simple flow that explains the value without any heavy UX diagrams.

  1. Select a destination or region.
  2. Check live weather and TOPR updates.
  3. Use the AI assistant to tailor the route (time, difficulty, experience).
  4. Save the plan and prepare with gear suggestions.
  5. Share tips and updates with the community.
